About NACOS LASU

The Nigeria Association of Computing Students (NACOS), LASU Chapter is the premier representative body for students within the Faculty of Computing and Information Technology. Dedicated to academic excellence and professional growth, the association serves as a vital bridge between theoretical classroom learning and the practical demands of the global tech industry.

Through a robust lineup of initiatives including hackathons, tech workshops, and mentorship programs NACOS-LASU empowers its members with essential digital skills and leadership training. By fostering a culture of innovation and continuous learning, the association ensures that students are not just technically proficient, but also prepared to tackle real-world challenges as impact-driven professionals.

Driven by a spirit of collaboration, NACOS-LASU actively networks with industry leaders to provide students with career opportunities and insider insights. As a forward-thinking community, it remains committed to shaping the next generation of tech innovators and thought leaders who will contribute meaningfully to the evolution of the national and global technological landscape.
